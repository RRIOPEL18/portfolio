The Dorsia Club

The Dorsia Club is a company founded by my close friend Matthew Mathers and I. Dorsia is an educational platform designed to inform visitors of our shared philosophies. Currently it has two pages (both written by Matthew); one outlining basic financial knowledge, and the other an elaborate consesus on modern physics. In the future we plan to produce a podcast and post episodes to the site. The podcast is mainly finance related, but will also discuss day to day life from the perspective of various guests.

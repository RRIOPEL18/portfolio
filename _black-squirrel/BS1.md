The Espresso Bar appears from the outside as just another place to get coffee. But once inside you realize it is a place to celebrate literature, art, culture, and creativity.
Thats just during the day.
At night events range from live music to stand up comedy, drawing in large crowds for a night of dynamic entertainment.<br>
In my opinion Black Squirrels current logo does not display the underlying sophistication and amusement associated with their brand.
